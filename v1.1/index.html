Tenant organizers have limited time and capacity. ANHD produces DAP District Reports to help them prioritize outreach. The reports give monthly updates on displacement risk to stabilized tenants in the neighborhoods where organizers work.

DAP District Reports provide a list of rent stabilized buildings by Community Board where tenants may be at risk of harassment and displacement. Risk is indicated by new property sales, HPD complaints, HPD violations, DOB complaints, and DOB construction permit applications.

Buildings that appear on multiple lists are coded yellow, orange, or red, depending on the level of risk.
