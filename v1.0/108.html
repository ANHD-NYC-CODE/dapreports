A monthly update of harassment and displacement risk in rent stabilized buildings in Manhattan Community Board 8. Risk indicators include

  1. New sales of residential buildings
  2. Five or more new complaints filed with the Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD)
  3. Ten or more new violations issued by HPD
  4. Two or more new complaints filed with the Department of Buildings (DOB)
  5. New DOB construction permit applications.

Buildings that appear on multiple lists are coded yellow, orange, or red, based on the number of lists they're on.

Links let you click through directly to a property's HPD building info, DOB's Building Information System, ACRIS financial records, or property tax information page. You can also click a building address to view it in Google Maps.

All buildings listed have registered stabilized units at least once between 2007 and 2016 (Notes).

NYS Housing and Community Renewal maintains the most accurate information on rent stabilization, which is not available to the public.
