You can also click the link for a building's registered Owner to view its Who Owns What page and its Address to view it in Google Maps. The (Taxes) link will take you to the Department of Finance portal to view tax records and tax exemption information.
If a building appears on more than one of the lists below in a given month it will be coded:
Yellow: two risk factors

Tip: to find the building on other lists, expand all of them and then copy its BBL (Borough, Block, and Lot code) and use Find (Ctrl+F or ⌘+F).
